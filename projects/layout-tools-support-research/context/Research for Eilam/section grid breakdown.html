Difficulty Adjusting Grid/Cell Sizing & Structure Users are encountering various limitations and difficulties with resizing and adjusting section grids and cells in Wix Studio. Many report being unable to change cell sizes due to fixed or locked grid templates, auto-sizing behaviors, or conflicting margin and padding settings. Users also struggle to add or split cells and rows, modify column layouts, and control height adjustments, often facing glitches where resize handles do not appear or changes revert unexpectedly. Additionally, users experience issues with grid structures causing unwanted white space, overlapping elements, and difficulties in adding new content like cards or podcast entries within grids.32

Responsive Design Difficulties & Breakpoint ManagementUsers are facing multiple responsiveness and layout issues across different breakpoints, especially on mobile and tablet views. Problems include misaligned elements, overlapping buttons, inconsistent cell ordering, and sections displaying incorrectly due to rigid or improper grid setups. Many users struggle to adjust grids from columns to rows, maintain proportional scaling, and handle sections that don’t adapt well to various screen sizes, causing white space, hidden content, and poor visual consistency between desktop and mobile versions.37

Overlapping Elements & Unwanted Spacing (Visual Clutter) Users face layout issues like shifting or overlapping elements, unwanted white space, and misaligned grids. These problems are caused by elements outside gridlines, locked grid sizes, improper padding/margins, and section heights. Responsive design often breaks due to grid constraints, layering errors, and difficulty adjusting spacing, especially on mobile. Many gaps appear only on live sites, stemming from grid and layout system limitations.37

Workflow Friction & User Confusion Users face difficulties with selecting and adjusting section grids, confusion about grid structure, containers, and galleries, and struggle with moving or swapping cells and elements within grids. They also experience issues with copying, duplicating, and managing section grids, plus challenges understanding tutorials and terminology around sections, containers, and grids.30
